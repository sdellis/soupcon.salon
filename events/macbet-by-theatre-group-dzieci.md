***Para-theatre Workshop***

**3:30pm** [Para-theatre ](http://dziecitheatre.org/the-work/workshops/)[workshop](http://dziecitheatre.org/the-work/workshops/) w​ith Theatre Group Dzieci, 1.5 hours.
